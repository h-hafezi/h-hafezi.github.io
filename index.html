I teach and research security engineering and applied cryptography. I'm currently most interested in verifiable lotteries, distributed randomness, cryptocurrencies, applications of cryptographic proofs, secure messaging protocols and HTTPS. I'm co-author of a textbook on cryptocurrencies which you can read for free online.
