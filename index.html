About Me

I'm a senior undergraduate student at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran, majoring in Mathematics and Applications. I've had the privilege of completing research internships under the supervision of Prof Bryan Ford at EPFL, Prof Ian Miers at the University of Maryland, and Prof Ghassan Karame at Ruhr University of Bochum.

My research interests are broad but mostly focus on building secure platforms using novel cryptographic primitives, especially SNARKs. Currently, I'm working with Prof Ian Miers (UMD) and Prof Pratyush Mishra (UPenn) working on scalability of SNARKs and a novel mechanism for a verifiable CPU.
