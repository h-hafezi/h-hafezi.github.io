About Me
I'm a senior undergraduate student at Sharif University of Technology in Tehran, majoring in Mathematics and Applications while minoring in Computer Science. I've had the privilege of completing research internships under Prof Bryan Ford at EPFL, Prof Ian Miers at UMD, and Prof Ghassan Karame at RUB.

My research interests are broad but mostly building secure platforms using novel cryptographic primitives, especially SNARKs. Presently, I'm engaged in collaborations with Prof Ian Miers (UMD) and Prof Pratyush Mishra (UPenn) working on scalibality of SNARKs and a novel mechanism for a verifiable CPU.
