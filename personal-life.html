My Hobbies

Chatting about the stuff I love to do, let's kick off with the active stuff like swimming, hiking, hitting the gym, bouldering, running, and now and then, gliding around on ice skates. Back in the day, I used to swim competitively, but now I'm all about that relaxed swim life. Though, I gotta say, I still daydream about smashing the 100m butterfly in less than a minute.

Then, when it comes to kicking back and watching sports, I'm all in for FC Bayern Munich since 2018. I've got a bit of a fan thing going for Manuel Neuer. And yeah, I'll catch a swimming event here and there, especially those national medley relays – they're just too cool.

As for my go-to TV shows, picking favorites is tough 'cause there's just so much good stuff. But, for sure, these make the list:

  • "24" – Caught this when I was just 12, and it's all about Jack Bauer being a total hero for his country. Though, I've got to be honest, I'm not cool with how it kinda throws shade at Muslims, Russians, and Chinese. Still love it, though.
  • "Modern Family"
  • "How I Met Your Mother"
  • "Gilmore Girls" – Never got to live through the '90s and '00s, but something about those times feels just right. Watching this show kinda takes me back, and it's hard to put into words how awesome that feels.
